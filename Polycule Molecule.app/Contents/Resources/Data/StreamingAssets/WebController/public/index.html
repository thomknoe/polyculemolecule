      How to play

      Description

      Welcome to Polycule Molecule! In this game, you are one of four players on the shared board and you move during courting, select personas in the molecule view, and press the love-language buttons in order to bond with personas and grow your polycule over the rounds. You’re not limited to strangers who show up each round! You can also court personas who already belong to another player’s polycule — get close, tap them in the molecule view to target, then spend your actions on whoever you picked.

      Actions

      Each courting round starts with 10 actions.

      Every time you press an action button, you spend one action. When you reach 0, you’re pulled back to your area and can’t walk out until that courting round ends. The next round refills 10 and you can move on the board again.

      Controls

      • Joystick — move your dot on the TV while you have actions and aren’t area-locked.
      • Molecule View — tap a persona to target them for court actions and breakups.
      • Six buttons — Affirm, Service, Gifts, Time, Touch, plus Break up on a selected partner you’re bonded to.
      • Resource meters — how much of each love language you can still spend; use them to court personas.

      Flip to vertical

      You are holding the phone horizontally. Rotate to portrait for the best experience.
